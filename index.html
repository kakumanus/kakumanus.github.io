1. About Me

I am a graduate of The Ohio State University, with a degree in Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence Specialization). In addition, I minored in City & Regional Planning. Post-graduation, I spent ~3.5 years as a Software Engineer at JPMorgan Chase, building a full-stack application for the commercial bank.

I am keenly interested in the intersection between Computer Science, Data Science, and City Planning. The rapid growth of technology is having previously unimaginable effects on our urban areas - both good and bad.

I believe that, if used carefully and methodically, data and software can empower local leaders and improve the lives of residents.
