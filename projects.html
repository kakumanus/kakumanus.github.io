Hack OHI/O 2018

In the Fall of 2018, I participated in Hack OHI/O - Ohio State's annual hackathon - with three other teammates. We pursued a challenge posed by Honda that revolved around data collected from their connected vehicle fleet. By the end of the 24 hour competition, we had developed the foundation of a navigation application for Android. This app uses a mix of data to present directional routes ranked by safety rather than speed. The app also contains a section where Honda's data is used to provide driving safety feedback for users. This app was chosen as the winner of the Honda Challenge.
